So you want a little backstory?

My name's Graham Bewley. I'm a web developer from Michigan. I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Saginaw Valley State University, where along with learning a lot of math and C++, I discovered a passion for working on the web.

Since graduating from college, I've added a few more skills to my toolset, learning React and Node, and building some hobby projects along the way. I've worked with a number of frameworks — like Gatsby and Next.js — along with useful web technologies — like WebSockets and MongoDB — to create functional and beautiful websites and apps.

While my focus is on all things JavaScript, I'm also familiar with advanced styling patterns using modern CSS, and the value of semantic and accessible HTML.

And when I'm away from my desk?

I love spending time with my wife, Jordan, and our two dogs, Chayse and Brooklyn. We try to get outside for some camping and hiking whenever we get the chance.

Otherwise, I spend much of my free time mountain biking, but also enjoy road biking, gravel riding — anything with two wheels and pedals. When we're stuck indoors, I do a bit of home brewing, and make a mean pale ale.
